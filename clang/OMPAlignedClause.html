class OMPAlignedClause

Defined at line 4181 of file /usr/lib/llvm-16/include/clang/AST/OpenMPClause.h

This represents clause 'aligned' in the '#pragma omp ...' directives.

In this example directive '#pragma omp simd' has clause 'aligned' with variables 'a', 'b' and alignment '8'.

Inherits from OMPVarListClause<OMPAlignedClause>, llvm::TrailingObjects<OMPAlignedClause, Expr *>
