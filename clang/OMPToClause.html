Defined at line 6734 of file /usr/lib/llvm-16/include/clang/AST/OpenMPClause.h

This represents clause 'to' in the '#pragma omp ...' directives.

In this example directive '#pragma omp target update' has clause 'to' with the variables 'a' and 'b'.
