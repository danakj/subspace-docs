class OMPTaskReductionClause

Defined at line 3417 of file /usr/lib/llvm-16/include/clang/AST/OpenMPClause.h

This represents clause 'task_reduction' in the '#pragma omp taskgroup' directives.

In this example directive '#pragma omp taskgroup' has clause 'task_reduction' with operator '+' and the variables 'a' and 'b'.

Inherits from OMPVarListClause<OMPTaskReductionClause>, OMPClauseWithPostUpdate , llvm::TrailingObjects<OMPTaskReductionClause, Expr *>
