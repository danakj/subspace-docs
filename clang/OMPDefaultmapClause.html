class OMPDefaultmapClause

Defined at line 6619 of file /usr/lib/llvm-16/include/clang/AST/OpenMPClause.h

This represents 'defaultmap' clause in the '#pragma omp ...' directive.

In this example directive '#pragma omp target' has 'defaultmap' clause of kind 'scalar' with modifier 'tofrom'.
