class OMPLinearClause

Defined at line 3904 of file /usr/lib/llvm-16/include/clang/AST/OpenMPClause.h

This represents clause 'linear' in the '#pragma omp ...' directives.

In this example directive '#pragma omp simd' has clause 'linear' with variables 'a', 'b' and linear step '2'.

Inherits from OMPVarListClause<OMPLinearClause>, OMPClauseWithPostUpdate , llvm::TrailingObjects<OMPLinearClause, Expr *>
