class OMPLastprivateClause

Defined at line 2770 of file /usr/lib/llvm-16/include/clang/AST/OpenMPClause.h

This represents clause 'lastprivate' in the '#pragma omp ...' directives.

In this example directive '#pragma omp simd' has clause 'lastprivate' with the variables 'a' and 'b'.

Inherits from OMPVarListClause<OMPLastprivateClause>, OMPClauseWithPostUpdate , llvm::TrailingObjects<OMPLastprivateClause, Expr *>
