ObjCBoxedExpr - used for generalized expression boxing. as in: @(strdup("hello world")), @(random()) or @(view.frame) Also used for boxing non-parenthesized numeric literals; as in: @42 or @true (c++/objc++) or @__objc_yes (c/objc).
