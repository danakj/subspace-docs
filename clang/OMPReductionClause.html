class OMPReductionClause

Defined at line 3081 of file /usr/lib/llvm-16/include/clang/AST/OpenMPClause.h

This represents clause 'reduction' in the '#pragma omp ...' directives.

In this example directive '#pragma omp parallel' has clause 'reduction' with operator '+' and the variables 'a' and 'b'.

Inherits from OMPVarListClause<OMPReductionClause>, OMPClauseWithPostUpdate , llvm::TrailingObjects<OMPReductionClause, Expr *>
