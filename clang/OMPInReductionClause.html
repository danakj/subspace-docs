class OMPInReductionClause

Defined at line 3648 of file /usr/lib/llvm-16/include/clang/AST/OpenMPClause.h

This represents clause 'in_reduction' in the '#pragma omp task' directives.

In this example directive '#pragma omp task' has clause 'in_reduction' with operator '+' and the variables 'a' and 'b'.

Inherits from OMPVarListClause<OMPInReductionClause>, OMPClauseWithPostUpdate , llvm::TrailingObjects<OMPInReductionClause, Expr *>
