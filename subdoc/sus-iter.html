BoxedIteratorType make_boxed_iterator (IteratorSubclass &&)

Make a BoxedIterator.

The BoxedIterator's internals will be on the heap, making the BoxedIterator itself be trivially relocatable, as it's just some pointers to the heap.

SizedIteratorType make_sized_iterator (IteratorSubclass &&)

Make a SizedIterator.

This overload is used when the IteratorSubclass can be trivially relocated. It stores the SubclassIterator directly into the SizedIterator, avoiding a heap allocation, since the SizedIterator can then be trivially relocated.
